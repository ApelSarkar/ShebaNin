About Electronic Service

The project Electronics Service is specially developed for General people of Bangladesh. There are many people in our country have many electronics devices like TV, Radio, Mobile Phone, Refrigerator, Monitor, Air-Condition etc. Sometimes these may have some problems. Then The customer need Electronic mechanic who can repair that device. And there are so many mechanics they do not have any job in market-place. They are searching for a job according to their profession. And here in this Software we are going to solve these problems. If the customer searching for a mechanic for any specific device he/she search engineer and engineer will response and solve the problem and get salary from the customer.
